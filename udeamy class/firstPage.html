CAVI Kitchen

Community ~ Sustainability ~ Health

CAVI Kitchen was started by two Pepperdine University students, Caroline and Victoria, who are passionate about sustainability, eating well and building community.

Caroline is a recent graduate from Pepperdine University, where she studied Computer Science and Business. Caroline is passionate about using technology to meet the needs of under resourced industries and is always up for an adventure.

Victoria is a rising senior at Pepperdine University, pursuing a degree in International Management and Hispanic Studies. Victoria is passionate about building engagement in food sustainability practices and loves to go on hikes whenever possible.
